TICKETS NOW ON SALE!

Tickets are now on sale for both Friday night, Saturday day and Saturday night, so get in touch ASAP to order your tickets and avoid the risk of disappointment.

Evening Tickets £6

An evening entry ticket will be just £6. Entry is adult only. Included as part of this price will be a free festival beer glass, and a programme with details of all the beers.

Day Tickets £3

An adult day entry ticket will be just £3, Children are welcome and entry for them is free. Included as part of this price will be a free festival beer glass, and a programme with details of all the beers.

We recommend purchasing tickets for all sessions in advance. Saturday afternoon tickets can be purchased on the door.
