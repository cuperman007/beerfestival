SNG Barratt Group Limited

Born from a love of Jaguar Cars over 30 years ago, SNG Barratt is the world’s largest independent supplier and manufacturer of parts and accessories for the marque. With a global span via their HQ in the UK and branches in the USA, Holland & France, there isn’t a part of the world that they can’t reach.

SNG Barratt’s range now cover every Jaguar model from 1949 to present day and their customers have unrivalled access to the part required, through the team of dedicated and knowledgeable salesmen. All day to day processes, from manufacturing through to sales and despatch, are compliant to ISO 9001 standards and are inspected annually. The UK branch is accredited by Jaguar as an Authorised Parts Dealership, allowing access to the full range of genuine Jaguar parts and resources. SNG Barratt’s quick and competitive shipping service means that parts can be delivered to your door.

SNG Barratt are not just parts suppliers, they also own several test vehicles for research and development. The opportunity to fit and fully test new parts helps them to deliver the very best to their customers, and paves the way for future enhancements, modifications and remanufacture to take place in-house.

The sales team are out on the road throughout the year at events ranging from club shows and hillclimbs, to international exhibitions, providing great offers and a popular order collection service. For a full list of upcoming shows see the Events section of this website.
